About this project

Agriculture is a vital sector in Egypt's economy, but many farmers struggle with the high costs of diesel-powered irrigation systems. Our solar-powered irrigation solution offers a sustainable alternative that reduces operating costs by up to 70% while being environmentally friendly.

With your support, we can install these systems in 50 farms across Upper Egypt, directly benefiting over 200 families and reducing carbon emissions by an estimated 120 tons annually.
